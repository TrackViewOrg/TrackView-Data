## TRACKVIEW Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 30, 2026

## 1. Controller Information

**Controller:** TRACKVIEW
**Jurisdiction:** Spain / European Union
**Website:** [https://trackview.app](https://trackview.app)
**Contact:** [support@trackview.app](mailto:support@trackview.app)

TRACKVIEW acts as the data controller for the purposes of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR). TRACKVIEW has not appointed a Data Protection Officer, as it is not legally required to do so under Article 37 GDPR.

## 2. Categories of Personal Data Collected

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

* **Identifiers:** Name, email address, country of residence (optional)
* **Account Data:** Account credentials, subscription status
* **Payment Data:** Billing status and transaction references (payment card data is processed exclusively by third‑party payment processors and is not stored by TRACKVIEW)
* **Telemetry and Usage Data:** Racing telemetry, vehicle configurations, performance metrics associated with user accounts
* **Technical Data:** IP address, operating system, application logs
* **Preference Data:** Favorite tracks, vehicles, and usage preferences

## 3. Methods of Collection

Personal data is collected through:

* Account registration and direct user input
* Use of TRACKVIEW software or related services
* Cookies and analytics technologies
* Customer support communications

## 4. Legal Bases for Processing

TRACKVIEW processes personal data under the following legal bases pursuant to Article 6 GDPR:

* **Performance of a contract** – provision of services, account management, telemetry processing
* **Consent** – marketing communications, non‑essential cookies, optional data
* **Legitimate interests** – service security, fraud prevention, internal analytics
* **Legal obligation** – accounting, tax, and regulatory compliance

## 5. Purposes of Processing

Personal data is processed to:

1. Provide and operate TRACKVIEW services
2. Manage accounts, subscriptions, and customer support
3. Improve performance, stability, and features through analytics
4. Send marketing communications where consent has been obtained
5. Comply with legal obligations and enforce terms

## 6. Information Sharing

* TRACKVIEW does **not sell personal data**.
* Personal data is shared only with:

  * Payment processors
  * Hosting, analytics, and infrastructure providers
* Such third parties act as **data processors** under GDPR and process data solely on TRACKVIEW’s instructions.
* Aggregated or anonymized data may be used for analytics, benchmarking, or research. De‑identification measures are applied, though absolute anonymity cannot be guaranteed.

## 7. Cookies and Analytics

TRACKVIEW uses cookies and similar technologies, including Google Analytics, to analyze usage and improve services.

* Non‑essential cookies are used only with user consent.
* Users may withdraw or modify cookie consent at any time through browser or platform controls.

## 8. User Rights

Users have the following rights under GDPR, subject to applicable law:

* Access to personal data
* Rectification of inaccurate or incomplete data
* Erasure of personal data
* Restriction of processing
* Data portability
* Objection to processing
* Withdrawal of consent where processing is based on consent

Requests may be submitted to [support@trackview.app](mailto:support@trackview.app) and will be addressed within **30 days**, unless legally extended.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Personal data may be processed outside the European Union. Where such transfers occur, they are safeguarded through:

* European Commission adequacy decisions, or
* Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission

## 10. Data Security

TRACKVIEW implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, including encryption in transit and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; however, reasonable safeguards are maintained.

## 11. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary:

* **Account data:** For the duration of the account and up to 2 years after deletion
* **Billing records:** As required by tax and accounting law (up to 10 years)
* **Telemetry and analytics:** Aggregated or deleted within 24 months
* **Support communications:** Up to 3 years

## 12. Children’s Privacy

TRACKVIEW does not knowingly process personal data of children under **14 years of age**, in accordance with Spanish law. If such data is identified, it will be deleted without delay.

## 13. Third‑Party Links

TRACKVIEW is not responsible for the privacy practices of third‑party websites or services linked from the platform.

## 14. Automated Decision‑Making

TRACKVIEW does not engage in automated decision‑making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects on users.

## 15. Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach, TRACKVIEW will notify relevant supervisory authorities within **72 hours** where required, and affected users without undue delay when legally mandated.

## 16. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes take effect upon publication with a revised “Last Updated” date.
