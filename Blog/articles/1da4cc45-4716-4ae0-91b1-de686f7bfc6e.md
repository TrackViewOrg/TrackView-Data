## From Raw Data to Professional Insights

The transformation of data follows a strict pipeline:

1. **Desktop Client (Tauri)**: Captures UDP packets from Assetto Corsa at 60Hz.
2. **Supabase**: Securely stores the high-frequency telemetry.
3. **Python (FastAPI)**: Executes the scientific models described above.
4. **Dashboard (Next.js & Recharts)**: Renders the results with smooth, 60fps animations.
